All About The Muscle

By Landen Worthy

Muscle cars are America's backbone of the car industry. Although comapared or seen as sports cars, they can be significantly different. A muscle car is a high performance vehichle that tyically comes packed with a powerful V8 engine. Although deemed to be lightweight some can come heavier than average sports cars.

They are designed to be very qucik, fast, and powerful and are dominant in striaght line performnace so are usually seen on the drag strip. The cost of a an muscle car can range from approximately $8,000 - $150,000. These prices are deemed to the cars performace, design, features, and age. The original muscle cars which were originated in the mid to late 60's and 70's were built on a basic intermediate body style (coupe) which came with 2 doors though had front as well as rear seats.
